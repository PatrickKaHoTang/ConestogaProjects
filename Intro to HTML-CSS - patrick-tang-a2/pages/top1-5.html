Here are my top 5 favourite video games of all time

#5 - Nosgoth

Nosgoth is one of the newer games I have played recently. For a game that I'm not too great at, I feel like it will become one of the better arena like games in the years to come. This game has a very competitive feel to it - something that I enjoy playing - and best of all, it's free!

#4 - Dransik

Dransik has been a game that I've been playing since 2002, on and off, I sometimes can't get enough of it! I go back to play it once in awhile just to have that competitive edge as it has a great battle system. Looks may be deceiving being it as it is a 2D game but once you play it at a competitive level, that adrenaline rush will surge through you to make you continue to play!

#3 - Rift

Rift, to me, is a free version of World of Warcraft. It has a great play style, lots of players who enjoy the same thing and you get to meet people from all over the world and become friends with them! I have definitely met a lot of great people on this game and I defintely enjoy playing a game that is free-to-play with a WoW feeling to it!

#2 - Path of Exile

Path of Exile is quite possibly one of the best ARPG games I have ever played! The leveling system is just like Diablo II but the skill system is something that no one has ever come up with before! The way you level is like the sphere grid from Final Fantasy X where you travel to get attributes that lets you build your character very uniquely.

#1 - World of Warcraft

World of Warcraft will most likely be in at least 90% of the gaming population's top video game they have ever played. As it is a starter game for most people who started doing hardcore gaming, this is by far the best game out there! Although I haven't played it in a long time due to the subscription, I would definitely play it again in a heartbeat!
